Our Story

A café born from
passion & craft

Nestled in the heart of Udaipur's historic Chandpole Chowk, Mavi Café is a sanctuary for those who seek more than just a coffee. We are artisans of flavour — roasting, brewing, and plating with intention.

From our hand-poured specialty coffees to our wholesome, seasonal food menu, every element is crafted to create an experience that lingers long after you leave. Earthy aesthetics, warm hospitality, and an unwavering commitment to quality define who we are.
